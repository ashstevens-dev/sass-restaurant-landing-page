Local, Feel Good Burgers

Our meats and produce are locally sourced and support PNW farmers! We only use pastured, organic, humanely treated pork, beef, and goat in our burgers, served on a freshly baked brioche bun, gluten-free bun, or romaine.

And for our non-carnivores and vegans we have an organic black bean & rice burger or the infamous Impossible burger, along with Nooch! We use organic olive or avocado oils in our cooking.

Why Goat Meat?

Studies show that goat meat is the 'healthy red meat' and that goats leave the land in better shape because they clear up shrubs and weeds. The flavor profile is not gamey - it's mild, somewhere between the flavor of pork and dark meat chicken.

We aim to rid the misconceptions about goat meat and make it more widely demanded and appreciated so that more farmers consider adding goat to their operations.
